Who We Are

Our Commitment: Reliable
and Efficient Logistics

Cold Sylos is an agritech logistics company pioneering sustainable cold chain infrastructure across Nigeria and Africa. With over 45% of Nigeria’s perishable produce lost to poor storage and transport, we are on a mission to build Africa’s most intelligent and accessible cold chain network — powered by clean energy and technology.

We reduce food waste, boost farmer incomes, and strengthen food security by delivering smart cold logistics at scale.

Our Vision & Mission

A Future Where Freshness Flows

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading provider of sustainable, intelligent cold chain logistics — ensuring perishable goods retain their value from farm to fork.

Our Mission

To deliver clean-energy-powered, tech-driven cold logistics that reduce food loss, empower rural communities, and build resilient agricultural value chains across the continent.
