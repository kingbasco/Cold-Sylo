Who We Are

Our Commitment: Reliable
and Efficient Logistics

Cold Sylos is an agritech logistics company building Africa’s most intelligent cold chain network. With over 45% of Nigeria’s perishable produce lost due to lack of cold storage and transport, we offer clean-energy-powered cold logistics that reduce food waste, empower farmers, and strengthen food security.

Our Service

What We Offer

At Cold Sylos, we provide smart, sustainable cold chain solutions tailored for Africa’s unique agricultural and logistics challenges. From farm to market, our services ensure that perishable goods stay fresh, valuable, and traceable every step of the way. Whether you’re a farmer, buyer, or logistics provider, our clean-energy infrastructure and digital tools empower you to reduce losses, increase efficiency, and grow your impact.

Cold Chain as a Service (CCaaS)

Affordable, pay-as-you-go refrigerated transport and storage.

Digital Aggregator Platform

A smart digital marketplace that connects farmers, buyers, and logistics operators in real time. Our platform enables seamless aggregation of produce, transparent pricing, and efficient matching of supply with demand.

Cold Storage Hubs

Our cold storage hubs provide safe, temperature-controlled environments for perishable goods, ensuring freshness and quality from farm to market.

CNG-Powered Cold Trucks

Our CNG-powered refrigerated trucks offer eco-friendly, cost-effective, and reliable cold transport for perishable goods, reducing emissions and ensuring freshness from origin to destination.

ColdChainOS (Coming Soon)

Tech for real-time tracking, inventory, and sustainability metrics.

Working Process

Efficient Working Process

We combine logistics expertise, clean energy, and smart tech to deliver a cold chain that works — seamlessly.

Request & Plan

Clients reach out or register through our platform. We assess their cold chain needs and create a customized service plan.

Schedule & Dispatch

Refrigerated transport and/or storage is scheduled. Our CNG-powered trucks and mobile units are dispatched on time.

Storage & Monitoring

Goods are stored in our solar-powered hubs or mobile units, with continuous temperature control and real-time monitoring.

Delivery & Insights

We deliver your goods fresh and intact. Clients get transparent delivery updates, inventory records, and sustainability reports.
